TEAM CHAAND

Collective Hive of Advanced Autonomous Navigation and Design

CHAAND (Collective Hive of Advanced Autonomous Navigation and Design) is a multi-disciplinary student team where engineering students across India came together to design a Lunar Rover and aid in future moon exploration.
The Moon aka Chaand has stolen our hearts! We have come up with an interesting design for a Swarm Rover System to serve our state of the art idea for mineral excavation and establishing a mesh of lunar communication-network. We divided ourselves into subsystems- Soft Landing, Mechanical, Power Systems and Communication, and Compute Module.
